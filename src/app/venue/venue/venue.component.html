From the park plaza hotel it is 4 minutes walk to the Central Station (Utrecht Central Station)

  • Walk to the south, in the direction of Jaarbeursplein.
  • Turn right to Jaarbeursplein.
  • Turn left to the Van Sijpesteijnkade.
  • Follow this route to the van Sijpestijntunnel.
  • Turn right.
  • You have arrived at Utrecht Centraal Station.

Please buy yourself a retour (return) ticket to train station Utrecht Leidsche Rijn Costs; € 4,60 for a return ticket, for 1 single trip € 2,30.

It will take 4 minutes by train from the Central Station To Utrecht Leidsche Rijn.

  • Behind the Leidsche Rijn station is the bus station.
  • Take bus 37 to Central station.
  • Leave the bus at Jaarbeurszijde.
  • Take bus 77 to P&R Westraven.
  • Leave the bus at P&R Westraven stop.
  • From here it is 3 minutes walk to the hotel (see map).
  • Cross the Griffioenlaan over the Europabaan.
  • You will reach the Mauritiuslaan.
