Westplein 50 3531 BL UTRECHT NETHERLANDS +31 30 292 5200

Hotel Park Plaza phone Call info Go to office

From the park plaza hotel it is 4 minutes walk to the Central Station (Utrecht Centraal)

  • Walk to the south, in the direction of Jaarbeursplein.
  • Turn right to Jaarbeursplein.
  • Turn left to the Van Sijpesteijnkade.
  • Follow this route to the van Sijpestijntunnel.
  • Turn right.
  • You have arrived at Utrecht Central Station.

Please get a retour (return) ticket to train station Utrecht Leidsche Rijn. It costs 2,30 € for a single trip and 4,60 € for a return ticket

It will take 4 minutes by train from the Central Station To Utrecht Leidsche Rijn.

  • Leave the bus at P&R Westraven stop.
